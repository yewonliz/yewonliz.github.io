Summary

⚠️ Spoiler alert! Are you sure you want to proceed? ⚠️

The Yi family – Jacob (Steven Yeun), Monica (Yeri Han), Anne (Noel Kate Cho), and David (Alan Kim) – move to a small town in Arkansas in 1983. Though Jacob is focused on growing Korean vegetables on his land, Monica is skeptical of his choice and wants to move to another town.

After a huge argument, the couple agrees on having Monica’s mom Soonja (Yuh Jung Youn) babysit the kids while they work. Soonja soon arrives with Korean food and minari seeds, planting them in the creek nearby. Though the children are initially reluctant to accept her as a “real grandmother”, Soonja strengthens their bond over time.

Unfortunately, she has a stroke during her sleep, which affects her movement and speech. While the rest of the family is away, Soonja accidentally tips over the can that is used to burn garbage. The fire spreads to the barn with all of Jacob’s harvests. Though they do not get to save the produce, none of them are hurt. The family ends us staying in the farm, and the movie ends with Jacob harvesting the minari at the creek.
